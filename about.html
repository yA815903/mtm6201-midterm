About Us

Dave Wild bakery has been serving delicious baked goods for over one year. Our mission is to provide our customers with the highest quality products and exceptional customer service.

Our History

Our bakery was founded in 2022 by Dave Wild, a passionate baker who wanted to share his love of baking with the world. Over the years, we have grown from a small family-owned bakery to a thriving business, but we have never lost sight of our commitment to quality and our customers.

Our Team

We have a team of skilled bakers and pastry chefs who are dedicated to creating delicious and unique baked goods. From our artisan bread to our beautiful wedding cakes, we take pride in every product we make.
